Baked Salmon

"insert paragraph long description of salmon that requires

miles of scrolling instead of just getting to the recipe."

Ingredients:

  • Literally salmon
  • Tonys Creole Seasoning
  • Lemon Juice

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Cut salmon to size
  3. Slap some Tony's Creole Seasoning and lemon juice on it
  4. Bake 15-18 minutes or until meat flakes off
  5. Eat it up
