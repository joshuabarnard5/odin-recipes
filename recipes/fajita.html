Ground Chicken Fajita

I make this for meal prep sometimes and it's a good way to

shake up the usual "chicken breast and white rice" menu.

I can typically eat this all week and be fine with it again for the next week.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs ground chicken
  • 3 large bell peppers of your color choice
  • White or brown rice
  • 1 taco seasoning packet

Steps:

  1. Put sliced bell peppers into air fryer for 13 minutes at 375 degrees
  2. Cook 2lbs ground chicken in large pan
  3. Once chicken is cooked stir in taco seasoning
  4. Cook rice
  5. Layer in bowl
